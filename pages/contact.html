Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take walk-ins?

While we welcome walk-ins, we highly recommend making a reservation to ensure availability, especially during peak hours and weekends.

What is your dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. We ask that guests avoid athletic wear, flip-flops, and overly casual attire.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can accommodate most dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and various allergies. Please inform us when making your reservation.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for our guests. Street parking is also available in the surrounding area.

Do you host private events?

Yes, we have private dining options for special events, corporate dinners, and celebrations. Please contact us directly to discuss your needs.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. For parties of 6 or more, we require 48 hours notice.
